Advancing AI Literacy in Integrative Medicine

Founded in 2025, the VUIM AI Lab is a collaborative, peer-led initiative dedicated to building a community of confident AI experimenters among faculty and staff. We focus on practical application and shared discovery to enhance our work and student services.

Our Mission

To empower VUIM faculty and staff with the practical AI skills and collaborative support needed to enhance productivity, improve student service, and drive institutional innovation from the ground up.

Our Approach

Our lab is built on a people-first philosophy.

Psychological Safety

All experience levels are welcome. We create a no-judgment zone where trying something new is a success and all questions are valued.

Voluntary Participation

Attendance and engagement are always voluntary. We respect everyone's time, priorities, and bandwidth, ensuring a no-pressure environment.

Peer-to-Peer Learning

We focus on collaborative problem-solving and sharing discoveries. Learning from each other's "jagged frontier" experiences is our curriculum.

Sustainable Growth

We start small and grow organically. The lab expands only when the community asks for more, ensuring our initiatives are always relevant and desired.
